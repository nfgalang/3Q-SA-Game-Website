About the Developers and Instructions

| About the Developers |

Yo, We are a group comprising of me(AJ), Nuncio, and Jaden, and we have made a game inspired by Galaga (and a bit of Wack-a-mole).

Game Programmer: Albert Jose L. Marcos

Game Graphics Designer: Nuntius Diego F. Galang

Game Ideater: Jaden Jose De Guzman

| Instructions |

1) Use your mouse to move sideways.

2) Press the screen to shoot.

3) Shoot the enemies to get points.

4) Get as much points before the timer runs out.

| Tips & Tricks |

1) Time your shots, time your shots to be more accurate,

2) Shoot as much as possible, shoot whenever you can, you only have a few seconds.

3) Shoot in rows, hit multiple enemies in a row.
